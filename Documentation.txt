   Welcome to the Oil Management System!
This program is designed to help you maintain, control and monitor the sales details, employee and 
customer details, profit/loss earned by your company. This system contains various features that are
mostly required to manage your Company.

The system provides various features which are listed as:
    1. Customer Management
    2. Employee Management
    3. Fuel Production Management
    4. Fuel Demand Management
    5. Profit/Loss Data along with graphs

You can also export the data you want into an External Excel Sheet for your mainpulation. The data can
be vey well added or deleted as per the user's demand. 

Please follow the further on-screen instructions and give input as per the prescibed format.
